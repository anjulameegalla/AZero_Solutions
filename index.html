Frequently Asked Questions

Career Launchpad

Is the CV compatible with ATS software?

Yes, absolutely. We strictly follow formatting rules (no graphics/tables) that ensure Applicant Tracking Systems can read and rank your application.

What is included in LinkedIn optimization?

We offer a full makeover including headline optimization for SEO, summary rewriting to tell your story, and alignment of your skills section to match your new CV.

What is GitHub optimization?

We structure your GitHub profile to highlight your best repositories, create a professional README.md with stats/skills, and ensure it appeals to tech recruiters.

What is the difference between a Cover Letter and Cover Email?

A Cover Letter is a formal document attached to applications. A Cover Email is a shorter, punchy version designed to be pasted directly into the body of an email for cold outreach.

Do you provide the editable file?

Yes! You will receive both the PDF version (for applying) and the editable Word (.docx) file so you can make future updates yourself.

How long does it take?

Standard delivery is between 3 to 5 days. Urgent 24-hour delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you write for specific industries?

We specialize in IT, Engineering, and Business roles, but our formats are universally accepted across all corporate sectors.

Can I see samples before ordering?

Yes, please contact us on WhatsApp, and we will share a portfolio of anonymized "Before & After" examples.

Do you offer refunds?

We do not offer refunds once work has been delivered, but we provide up to 2 rounds of free revisions to ensure you are happy with the result.

IT Support Essentials

How do you install software remotely?

We use secure screen-sharing tools (like AnyDesk or TeamViewer). You can watch the entire process while we configure the software for you.

Do you help with Microsoft 365?

Yes, we can help you set up accounts, install Office apps (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), and troubleshoot login issues.

Do you do complex coding or development?

No, our IT support is focused on "Small Tasks" ‚Äî installations, account setups, and general PC troubleshooting. We do not handle large-scale development projects.

Is using AnyDesk safe?

Yes. We only access your computer with your permission, and you can see everything we do. You can disconnect the session at any time.

How is payment calculated?

We charge a fixed fee for standard installations or an hourly rate for longer troubleshooting sessions.

Can you help with Mac and Windows?

Yes, we support both Windows (10/11) and macOS environments for general software and account setups.

What internet speed do I need?

A stable internet connection (at least 5-10 Mbps) is required for us to control your screen without lag.

Do you remove viruses?

We can perform basic malware scans and removal using standard tools, but severe infections may require a fresh OS installation.

Do you sell software keys?

No, we do not sell software licenses. We help you install software you have purchased or assist you in setting up free/open-source alternatives.
